Wimberley is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Jewel of the Hill Country," this hill country gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Wimberley is an artsy Hill Country escape with crystal-clear Jacobs Well, charming shops, and Blue Hole Regional Park.

## Things to Do in Wimberley

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Wimberley has something for everyone.

- **Swim at Jacobs Well** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Wimberley on the map.
- **Browse Wimberley Square shops** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Hike Blue Hole Regional Park** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Wimberley

Texas cuisine shines in Wimberley. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $40-$130 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Wimberley

Accommodations range from budget options ($40/night) to mid-range hotels ($130/night) to luxury properties ($300/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Wimberley

The best months to visit are March through October when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Wimberley

Drive from Austin (45 min) or San Antonio (60 min) via RR 12. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
