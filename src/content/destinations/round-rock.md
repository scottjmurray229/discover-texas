## What Makes Round Rock Special

Round Rock is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Sports and Family Fun Hub," this central texas gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Round Rock is Austins thriving neighbor with Dell Diamond, outlet shopping, and family-friendly attractions.

## Things to Do in Round Rock

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Round Rock has something for everyone.

- **Catch a game at Dell Diamond** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Round Rock on the map.
- **Shop at Round Rock Premium Outlets** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Swim at Old Settlers Park** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Round Rock

Texas cuisine shines in Round Rock. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $40-$110 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Round Rock

Accommodations range from budget options ($40/night) to mid-range hotels ($110/night) to luxury properties ($220/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Round Rock

The best months to visit are March through November when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Round Rock

Located 20 minutes north of Austin off I-35. Fly into Austin-Bergstrom (AUS). Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
