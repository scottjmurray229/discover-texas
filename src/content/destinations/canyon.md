## What Makes Canyon Special

Canyon is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Panhandle Plains History," this panhandle gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Canyon is home to the Panhandle-Plains Historical Museum, the largest history museum in Texas.

## Things to Do in Canyon

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Canyon has something for everyone.

- **Visit the Panhandle-Plains Historical Museum** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Canyon on the map.
- **Explore West Texas A&M campus** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Drive to Palo Duro Canyon** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Canyon

Texas cuisine shines in Canyon. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $25-$75 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Canyon

Accommodations range from budget options ($25/night) to mid-range hotels ($75/night) to luxury properties ($160/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Canyon

The best months to visit are April through October when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Canyon

Drive from Amarillo (15 min) via I-27. Fly into Amarillo (AMA). Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
