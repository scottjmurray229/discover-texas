## What Makes Glen Rose Special

Glen Rose is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Dinosaur Valley and Safari Country," this north texas gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Glen Rose is where you walk in actual dinosaur tracks at Dinosaur Valley State Park plus Fossil Rim Wildlife Center.

## Things to Do in Glen Rose

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Glen Rose has something for everyone.

- **Walk in dinosaur tracks at Dinosaur Valley** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Glen Rose on the map.
- **Drive through Fossil Rim Wildlife Center** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Swim in the Paluxy River** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Glen Rose

Texas cuisine shines in Glen Rose. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $35-$100 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Glen Rose

Accommodations range from budget options ($35/night) to mid-range hotels ($100/night) to luxury properties ($240/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Glen Rose

The best months to visit are March through October when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Glen Rose

Drive from Fort Worth (75 min) via US-67. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
