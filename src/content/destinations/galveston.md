## What Makes Galveston Special

Galveston is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Texas Beach Getaway," this gulf coast gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Galveston Island offers beaches, Victorian architecture, a lively Strand District, and fresh Gulf seafood an hour from Houston.

## Things to Do in Galveston

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Galveston has something for everyone.

- **Walk the historic Strand District** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Galveston on the map.
- **Visit Moody Gardens** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Relax on Stewart Beach** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Galveston

Texas cuisine shines in Galveston. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $45-$130 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Galveston

Accommodations range from budget options ($45/night) to mid-range hotels ($130/night) to luxury properties ($300/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Galveston

The best months to visit are March through October when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Galveston

Drive from Houston (60 min) via I-45 South. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
