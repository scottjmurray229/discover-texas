## What Makes Jefferson Special

Jefferson is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Haunted Bayou Town," this east texas gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Jefferson is the most haunted small town in Texas with antebellum mansions, bayou tours, and ghost walks.

## Things to Do in Jefferson

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Jefferson has something for everyone.

- **Take a haunted history tour** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Jefferson on the map.
- **Cruise Big Cypress Bayou** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Tour the antebellum mansions** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Jefferson

Texas cuisine shines in Jefferson. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $30-$90 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Jefferson

Accommodations range from budget options ($30/night) to mid-range hotels ($90/night) to luxury properties ($200/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Jefferson

The best months to visit are March through November when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Jefferson

Drive from Dallas (2.5 hours) via I-30 and US-59. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
