## What Makes Granbury Special

Granbury is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Historic Square on the Lake," this north texas gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Granburys preserved courthouse square overlooks a beautiful lake with great restaurants and real Texas charm.

## Things to Do in Granbury

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Granbury has something for everyone.

- **Explore the Granbury Square** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Granbury on the map.
- **Boat on Lake Granbury** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Catch a show at Granbury Opera House** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Granbury

Texas cuisine shines in Granbury. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $35-$100 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Granbury

Accommodations range from budget options ($35/night) to mid-range hotels ($100/night) to luxury properties ($240/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Granbury

The best months to visit are March through November when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Granbury

Drive from Fort Worth (45 min) via US-377. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
