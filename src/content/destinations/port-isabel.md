## What Makes Port Isabel Special

Port Isabel is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Gateway to South Padre," this gulf coast gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Port Isabel is the charming mainland gateway to South Padre Island with the historic lighthouse and fresh seafood.

## Things to Do in Port Isabel

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Port Isabel has something for everyone.

- **Climb the Port Isabel Lighthouse** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Port Isabel on the map.
- **Eat fresh seafood on the bay** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Kayak the Laguna Madre** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Port Isabel

Texas cuisine shines in Port Isabel. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $30-$90 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Port Isabel

Accommodations range from budget options ($30/night) to mid-range hotels ($90/night) to luxury properties ($200/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Port Isabel

The best months to visit are March through November when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Port Isabel

Fly into Valley International Airport (HRL) in Harlingen, 25 min drive. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
