## What Makes Abilene Special

Abilene is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Storybook Capital of America," this west texas gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Abilene is the Storybook Capital of America with Frontier Texas, the Abilene Zoo, and deep frontier heritage.

## Things to Do in Abilene

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Abilene has something for everyone.

- **Visit Frontier Texas!** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Abilene on the map.
- **Explore Abilene State Park** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Tour the Grace Museum** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Abilene

Texas cuisine shines in Abilene. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $30-$85 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Abilene

Accommodations range from budget options ($30/night) to mid-range hotels ($85/night) to luxury properties ($200/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Abilene

The best months to visit are March through November when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Abilene

Fly into Abilene Regional Airport (ABI) or drive from Dallas (2.5 hours) via I-20. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
