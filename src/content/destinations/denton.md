## What Makes Denton Special

Denton is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "College Town Cool," this north texas gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Denton is a creative college town with a thriving music scene, great coffee shops, and UNT campus culture.

## Things to Do in Denton

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Denton has something for everyone.

- **Explore the Courthouse Square** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Denton on the map.
- **Catch live music at Dans Silverleaf** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Shop the vintage stores** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Denton

Texas cuisine shines in Denton. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $30-$90 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Denton

Accommodations range from budget options ($30/night) to mid-range hotels ($90/night) to luxury properties ($200/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Denton

The best months to visit are March through November when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Denton

Drive from Dallas (45 min) via I-35E. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
