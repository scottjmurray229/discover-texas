## What Makes Surfside Beach Special

Surfside Beach is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Relaxed Beach Vibes Near Houston," this gulf coast gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Surfside Beach is Houstons closest beach escape with uncrowded sand and affordable beachfront rentals.

## Things to Do in Surfside Beach

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Surfside Beach has something for everyone.

- **Surf the Gulf waves** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Surfside Beach on the map.
- **Drive on the beach** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Eat at beachfront restaurants** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Surfside Beach

Texas cuisine shines in Surfside Beach. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $30-$85 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Surfside Beach

Accommodations range from budget options ($30/night) to mid-range hotels ($85/night) to luxury properties ($200/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Surfside Beach

The best months to visit are March through October when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Surfside Beach

Drive from Houston (75 min) via TX-288 and TX-332. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
