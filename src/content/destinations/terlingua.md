## What Makes Terlingua Special

Terlingua is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Ghost Town Revival," this west texas gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Terlingua is a former mercury mining ghost town reborn as the gateway to Big Bend with legendary chili cook-offs.

## Things to Do in Terlingua

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Terlingua has something for everyone.

- **Explore the ghost town ruins** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Terlingua on the map.
- **Drink a beer at the Starlight Theatre** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Raft the Rio Grande through Santa Elena Canyon** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Terlingua

Texas cuisine shines in Terlingua. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $30-$90 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Terlingua

Accommodations range from budget options ($30/night) to mid-range hotels ($90/night) to luxury properties ($220/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Terlingua

The best months to visit are October through April when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Terlingua

Drive from Alpine (80 min) via TX-118. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
