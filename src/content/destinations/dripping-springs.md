## What Makes Dripping Springs Special

Dripping Springs is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Gateway to the Hill Country," this hill country gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Dripping Springs is Austins craft beverage neighbor with distilleries, breweries, wineries, and Hamilton Pool nearby.

## Things to Do in Dripping Springs

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Dripping Springs has something for everyone.

- **Swim at Hamilton Pool Preserve** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Dripping Springs on the map.
- **Tour the distillery trail** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Visit Mercer Street Dance Hall** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Dripping Springs

Texas cuisine shines in Dripping Springs. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $45-$140 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Dripping Springs

Accommodations range from budget options ($45/night) to mid-range hotels ($140/night) to luxury properties ($320/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Dripping Springs

The best months to visit are March through November when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Dripping Springs

Located 25 miles west of Austin via US-290. Fly into Austin-Bergstrom (AUS). Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
