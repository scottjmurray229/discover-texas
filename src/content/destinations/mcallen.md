McAllen is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Heart of the Rio Grande Valley," this south texas gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

McAllen is the heart of the Rio Grande Valley with world-class birding, authentic Mexican cuisine, and warm winters.

## Things to Do in McAllen

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, McAllen has something for everyone.

- **Bird at Quinta Mazatlan** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts McAllen on the map.
- **Shop at La Plaza Mall** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Eat authentic Mexican food** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in McAllen

Texas cuisine shines in McAllen. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $30-$85 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in McAllen

Accommodations range from budget options ($30/night) to mid-range hotels ($85/night) to luxury properties ($200/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit McAllen

The best months to visit are November through March when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to McAllen

Fly into McAllen Miller International Airport (MFE). Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
