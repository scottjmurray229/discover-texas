## What Makes McKinney Special

McKinney is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Historic Charm North of Dallas," this north texas gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

McKinneys historic downtown is one of the best in Texas with boutique shopping and craft breweries.

## Things to Do in McKinney

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, McKinney has something for everyone.

- **Walk Historic Downtown McKinney** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts McKinney on the map.
- **Visit Chestnut Square Heritage Village** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Hike at Erwin Park** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in McKinney

Texas cuisine shines in McKinney. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $35-$110 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in McKinney

Accommodations range from budget options ($35/night) to mid-range hotels ($110/night) to luxury properties ($250/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit McKinney

The best months to visit are March through November when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to McKinney

Drive from Dallas (35 min) via US-75. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
