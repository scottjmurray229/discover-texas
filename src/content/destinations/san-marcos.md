## What Makes San Marcos Special

San Marcos is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Float the Crystal-Clear River," this central texas gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

San Marcos is a college town paradise with the crystal-clear San Marcos River, outlet malls, and vibrant nightlife.

## Things to Do in San Marcos

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, San Marcos has something for everyone.

- **Float the San Marcos River** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts San Marcos on the map.
- **Shop at San Marcos Premium Outlets** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Hike at Purgatory Creek Natural Area** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in San Marcos

Texas cuisine shines in San Marcos. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $35-$100 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in San Marcos

Accommodations range from budget options ($35/night) to mid-range hotels ($100/night) to luxury properties ($200/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit San Marcos

The best months to visit are April through October when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to San Marcos

Located between Austin and San Antonio on I-35, 30 min from Austin-Bergstrom (AUS). Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
