## What Makes Midland-Odessa Special

Midland-Odessa is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Oil Patch Heritage," this west texas gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Midland-Odessa is the heart of the Permian Basin oil patch with the Petroleum Museum and Bush Childhood Home.

## Things to Do in Midland-Odessa

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Midland-Odessa has something for everyone.

- **Visit the Petroleum Museum** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Midland-Odessa on the map.
- **Tour the George W. Bush Childhood Home** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **See the Odessa Meteor Crater** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Midland-Odessa

Texas cuisine shines in Midland-Odessa. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $35-$100 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Midland-Odessa

Accommodations range from budget options ($35/night) to mid-range hotels ($100/night) to luxury properties ($220/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Midland-Odessa

The best months to visit are October through April when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Midland-Odessa

Fly into Midland International Air and Space Port (MAF). Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
