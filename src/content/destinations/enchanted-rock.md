## What Makes Enchanted Rock Special

Enchanted Rock is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Sacred Pink Granite Dome," this hill country gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Enchanted Rock is a massive pink granite dome rising from the Hill Country, sacred to Native Americans and a must-hike.

## Things to Do in Enchanted Rock

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Enchanted Rock has something for everyone.

- **Summit the granite dome** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Enchanted Rock on the map.
- **Stargaze at the Dark Sky Park** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Explore the Echo Canyon trail** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Enchanted Rock

Texas cuisine shines in Enchanted Rock. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $30-$100 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Enchanted Rock

Accommodations range from budget options ($30/night) to mid-range hotels ($100/night) to luxury properties ($250/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Enchanted Rock

The best months to visit are March through November when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Enchanted Rock

Drive from Fredericksburg (18 min) via RR 965 or from Austin (90 min). Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
