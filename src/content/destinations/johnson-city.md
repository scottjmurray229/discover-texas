## What Makes Johnson City Special

Johnson City is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "LBJ Country," this hill country gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Johnson City is LBJs hometown with the presidential ranch, local wineries, and small-town Texas at its finest.

## Things to Do in Johnson City

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Johnson City has something for everyone.

- **Tour LBJ Ranch and Boyhood Home** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Johnson City on the map.
- **Visit Pedernales Falls State Park** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Sample wines on the 290 Wine Trail** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Johnson City

Texas cuisine shines in Johnson City. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $35-$110 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Johnson City

Accommodations range from budget options ($35/night) to mid-range hotels ($110/night) to luxury properties ($250/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Johnson City

The best months to visit are March through November when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Johnson City

Drive from Austin (50 min) via US-290, between Austin and Fredericksburg. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
