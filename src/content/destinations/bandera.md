Bandera is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Cowboy Capital of the World," this hill country gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Bandera is the real-deal cowboy town with dude ranches, honky-tonks, horseback riding, and the Medina River.

## Things to Do in Bandera

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Bandera has something for everyone.

- **Stay at a working dude ranch** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Bandera on the map.
- **Dance at the 11th Street Cowboy Bar** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Tube the Medina River** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Bandera

Texas cuisine shines in Bandera. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $40-$120 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Bandera

Accommodations range from budget options ($40/night) to mid-range hotels ($120/night) to luxury properties ($280/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Bandera

The best months to visit are March through November when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Bandera

Drive from San Antonio (50 min) via TX-16 in the western Hill Country. Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
