Corpus Christi is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Sparkling City by the Sea," this gulf coast gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Corpus Christi is a coastal gem with the USS Lexington, Padre Island National Seashore, and the Texas State Aquarium.

## Things to Do in Corpus Christi

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Corpus Christi has something for everyone.

- **Tour the USS Lexington** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Corpus Christi on the map.
- **Drive Padre Island National Seashore** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Visit the Texas State Aquarium** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Corpus Christi

Texas cuisine shines in Corpus Christi. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $40-$110 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Corpus Christi

Accommodations range from budget options ($40/night) to mid-range hotels ($110/night) to luxury properties ($260/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Corpus Christi

The best months to visit are March through November when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Corpus Christi

Fly into Corpus Christi International Airport (CRP) or drive from San Antonio (2.5 hours). Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
