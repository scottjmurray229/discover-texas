## What Makes Rockport-Fulton Special

Rockport-Fulton is one of Texas's most beloved destinations. Known as the "Artists and Birders Paradise," this gulf coast gem delivers authentic Lone Star experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Rockport-Fulton is a charming coastal arts colony with world-class birding at Aransas National Wildlife Refuge.

## Things to Do in Rockport-Fulton

Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or great food, Rockport-Fulton has something for everyone.

- **See whooping cranes at Aransas NWR** — A must-do for any visitor. This is the experience that puts Rockport-Fulton on the map.
- **Explore the Rockport Art Center** — Another highlight that showcases the best of the area.
- **Fish Copano Bay** — Round out your visit with this local favorite.

## Where to Eat in Rockport-Fulton

Texas cuisine shines in Rockport-Fulton. From legendary BBQ joints to Tex-Mex favorites and local specialties, you'll eat well here. Budget about $35-$100 per person per day for food.

## Where to Stay in Rockport-Fulton

Accommodations range from budget options ($35/night) to mid-range hotels ($100/night) to luxury properties ($240/night). Book ahead during peak season and major events.

## Best Time to Visit Rockport-Fulton

The best months to visit are October through April when temperatures are comfortable and outdoor activities are at their best. Summer months can be extremely hot across most of Texas.

## Getting to Rockport-Fulton

Drive from Corpus Christi (35 min) via TX-35 or from Houston (3.5 hours). Car rental is the most practical way to explore the area.
